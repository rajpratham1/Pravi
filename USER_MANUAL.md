# Pravi Marketplace - User Manual

Welcome to Pravi! This guide will help you navigate the platform, whether you are looking to buy a ready-made website or sell your own projects.

---

## 1. General Features

### Account Creation
-   **Sign Up:** Click "Login/Signup" to create a new account. You can register as a **User** (Buyer) or apply to become a **Seller**.
-   **Dark Mode:** Toggle the switch in the navigation bar to switch between Light and Dark themes.

---

## 2. For Buyers

### Finding a Website
-   **Browse:** Visit the "Listings" page to see all available websites.
-   **Search & Filter:** Use the search bar to find specific keywords. Use the "Sort By" dropdown to organize by Price (Low/High) or Newest.
-   **View Details:** Click on any card to see the full description, live demo link, and seller contact info.

### Making a Purchase
1.  **Add to Cart:** Click "Add to Cart" on the listing page.
2.  **Checkout:** Go to your Cart and click "Proceed to Checkout".
3.  **Discounts:** Have a code? Enter `PRAVI10` for a 10% discount!
4.  **Payment:** We support **UPI Payments**. Click "Pay Now" to open your UPI app or scan the QR code.
5.  **Orders:** Once paid, track your purchase in the "Dashboard" under "My Orders".

---

## 3. For Sellers

### Becoming a Seller
To ensure quality, all sellers must be verified.
1.  Go to "Login/Signup" and select the **Seller** tab.
2.  Fill out the **Application Form**.
3.  **Upload Documents:** You must upload a clear copy of your **Aadhaar Card** and **PAN Card** for KYC.
4.  **Wait for Approval:** An admin will review your documents. You cannot log in as a seller until approved.

### Managing Listings
-   **Create Listing:** Once approved, log in to your Seller Dashboard and click "Create Listing".
-   **Details:** Upload a screenshot, set a price, and provide the website URL.
-   **Manage:** You can Edit or Delete your listings anytime from the dashboard.
-   **Inquiries:** Check the "Inquiries" section to see messages from interested buyers.

---

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Is my payment secure?**
A: Yes, payments are processed directly via secure UPI apps.

**Q: How do I deliver the website code?**
A: Once a buyer purchases your site, you (the seller) should contact them via the email provided in the order to transfer files/access.

**Q: Can I refund a purchase?**
A: Please contact support for refund requests.
